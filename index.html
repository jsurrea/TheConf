Meet the

Speakers

Steve Jobs
Apple

Co-founder of Apple, one of the world's most valuable and innovative technology companies. Jobs was a pioneer in the personal computer industry and is widely recognized for his design and marketing genius.

Bill Gates
Microsoft

Co-founder of Microsoft, one of the world's largest software companies. Gates is also known for his philanthropic efforts, including the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation, which focuses on global health and education initiatives.

Larry Page
Google

Co-founder of Google, one of the world's most widely used search engines. Page is a tech entrepreneur and philanthropist who has also founded companies such as Alphabet Inc. and Google X, which focus on innovative projects such as self-driving cars and healthcare technology.

Mark Zuckerberg
Meta

Co-founder and CEO of Facebook, the world's largest social media platform. Zuckerberg is known for his entrepreneurial spirit and his vision of connecting people through technology.

Elon Musk
Tesla

Founder and CEO of Tesla, Inc., SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company. Musk is a pioneer in the fields of electric cars, space exploration, and renewable energy, and has been named one of the most influential people in the world.

Jeff Bezos
Amazon

Founder and former CEO of Amazon, the world's largest online retailer. Bezos is a visionary leader who has transformed the retail industry and is also known for his space exploration company, Blue Origin.

Honolulu - October 2023

Honolulu, Hawaii is the perfect destination for TheConf event. Attendees can enjoy stunning beaches, cultural attractions, delicious cuisine, outdoor adventures, and the warm hospitality of the local people. This vibrant city on the island of Oahu is the ideal backdrop for a transformative experience that will leave attendees inspired and energized.
